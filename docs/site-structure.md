# Site Structure

This document describes the current structure and purpose of the portfolio website.

## Home page

The home page introduces Gregory Brouzoukis and gives a first impression of the website. It presents the main direction of the portfolio and guides visitors toward the most important sections.

The page is designed to look clean, modern and focused on technology.

## Professional profile

This section explains the basic identity of the portfolio. It presents the work areas connected with web development, programming, applications and AI automation.

The goal is to make the profile clear for visitors who want to understand what the website is about without reading too much text.

## Projects

The projects section is planned to become one of the most important parts of the website.

It will include personal work, programming projects, websites, small applications and technical experiments. Each project should eventually have a short description, the tools used, the problem it solves and what was learned from it.

## Reviews

The reviews section is used to show feedback in a clean and professional way.

The styling was adjusted with custom CSS so that it fits better with the dark design of the website.

## Blog area

The blog area exists as a future content section. It can be used later for articles about programming, web development, AI tools, learning progress and project updates.

For now, the blog section can stay hidden or limited until the content is ready.

## Legal pages

The website includes legal and trust pages such as privacy policy, cookie policy and terms of use.

These pages help the website look more complete and professional, while also supporting safer public use.

## Contact area

The contact area gives visitors a way to reach out for communication, questions or project interest.

The goal is to keep the contact process simple and clear.
